Water Pollution

Protecting Our Most Precious Resource

Understanding Water Pollution

Water pollution occurs when harmful substances contaminate water bodies, degrading water quality and making it toxic to humans and the environment.

The Water Cycle and Pollution

Pollution can disrupt the natural water cycle, affecting ecosystems and human health at every stage.

Major Sources of Water Pollution

  • Industrial waste
  • Agricultural runoff
  • Plastic waste
  • Oil spills
  • Sewage and wastewater

Environmental Impacts

  • Destruction of aquatic ecosystems
  • Reduction of biodiversity
  • Contamination of drinking water
  • Marine life endangerment
  • Global climate change effects

How Can We Help?

Individuals can make a difference by:

  • Reducing plastic use
  • Proper disposal of chemicals
  • Supporting clean water initiatives
  • Conserving water
  • Participating in local cleanup efforts
